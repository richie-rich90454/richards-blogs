Well... Who am I?

I am Richard Jiang. I would say I am a student, and I would probably consider my self to be a person who is rather interested in a few topics, including science (chemistry and some fields of physics and biology), computer science (both hardware and software), and social studies (history). I often dive deep into such topics in my non-cirricular times, and I really enjoy being an expert in a certain field.

Okay... Who are your friends?

My friends are often like me. My friends are also experts of "niches", and we often become friends due to our same interest in an area. For instance, many friends I had in Middle School were known by me due to them either being knowledgable of computers (computer science) or aviation (fluid dynamics are a key part of aviation, and fluid dynamics are an integral part of physics).
